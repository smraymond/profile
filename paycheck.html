Paycheck Pickup

Temporarily Available Here

My Paycheck Pickup web app is an application currently being used by the College of Arts and Letters at CSUSB, to digitally record when the faculty, staff, and student assistants pickup their paychecks, with a signature.

Picking Up

When a department is selected, the faculty and staff are listed, as shown here. Several can be selected at once, and be signed for by a single individual. Once signed, the time is recorded and saved in the database.

Recording Signature

jSignature is used to capture the signature and store it in the database as an svg. Once the paycheck is signed for, it is saved in the history, which can then be viewed and printed as a PDF, as shown below.
