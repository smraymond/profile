Student Timesheets

Temporarily Available Here

This is a web application I built for my work as an IT Student Assistant. It allows students to log their hours for the current pay period, and then print out a PDF version of our timesheets.

Log Hours

These hours are logged by day, allowing up to two entries. These times are then printed on the PDF in their appropriate cells using FreePDF. New entries can be added, for instances where the pay period ends after timesheets are due. All clock-ins are also rounded to every tenth hours, or six minutes.

Profile Information

In order to calculate the salary, the timesheet requires information about the student. Including job title, hourly wage, and class schedule. Once the hourly wage is known, the timesheet will multiply it by the total hours worked, to gather the monthly salary. Seen below is an example PDF, using the information seen above.
